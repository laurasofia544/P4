My Library

Reminders of Him

After serving five years in prison for a tragic mistake, Kenna Rowan returns to the town where it all went wrong, hoping to reunite with her four-year-old daughter. But the bridges Kenna burned are proving impossible to rebuild. Everyone in her daughter's life is determined to shut Kenna out, no matter how hard she works to prove herself.

The Seven Husbands of Evelyn Hugo

The story revolves around the fictional Hollywood icon, Evelyn Hugo, who, in her old age, decides to tell her life story to an unknown journalist named Monique Grant. Evelyn Hugo has had a highly publicized and glamorous life, but her true motives for choosing Monique become clear as she reveals the intricate details of her seven marriages.

The Invisible Life of Addie LaRue

The story follows Adeline "Addie" LaRue, a young woman in 18th-century France who makes a Faustian bargain to live forever but is cursed to be forgotten by everyone she meets. As a result, she navigates through centuries, leaving no lasting mark on the world and experiencing the isolation of her immortal, invisible life. However, Addie's existence takes a turn when, after 300 years, she encounters a man who remembers her.
